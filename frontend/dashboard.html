Onboarding Guide

Master the AI Trader Setup

Recommendation

Start with Paper Trading

We strongly recommend all beginners start with a **Paper Account**. This uses $100,000 of "fake money" to simulate real market conditions without risking your capital.

1
Create Alpaca Account

Sign up at Alpaca.markets and verify your email.

2
Choose Environment

In your Alpaca dashboard, toggle to **"Paper Trading"** in the sidebar.

3
Generate API Keys

Look for the **"API Keys"** section and click "Generate New Key". You will get an **API Key ID** and a **Secret Key**.

4
Connect to Bot

Paste those keys into the "Link Account" window here. Make sure the "Paper Trading" toggle matches your Alpaca dashboard setting.

Why Paper First?

  • ✔ master indicator confluence
  • ✔ test different timeframes
  • ✔ build confidence in AI signals
