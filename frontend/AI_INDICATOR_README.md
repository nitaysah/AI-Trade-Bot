# BotBulls AI Indicator Card

This document provides a detailed overview of the **AI Indicator Card** component integrated into the BotBulls trading dashboard. The AI Indicator combines technical analysis, macro data, options flow, and financial news into a single, unified trade thesis generated by Groq's LLaMA 3 models.

## Overview
The AI Indicator fetches structured context (Webull bars, technicals like RSI/MACD, implied volatility, macro regimes, and recent headlines) and requests an analysis from a Large Language Model (`llama-3.3-70b-versatile` with an automatic fallback to `llama-3.1-8b-instant` if rate limits are hit). 

This analysis is surfaced on the frontend as a comprehensive, modular summary card designed for at-a-glance decision making.

## Component Breakdown

### 1. Decision Confidence
A quantitative confidence score assigned by the AI model on its chosen trade direction (BUY, SELL, or HOLD).
- **Scale**: 0% to 100%
- **Color Mapping**:
  - **`> 70%`**: 🟢 Emerald (`bg-emerald-500`) - High confidence in the signal.
  - **`45% - 69%`**: 🟠 Amber (`bg-amber-500`) - Moderate/Warning confidence.
  - **`< 45%`**: 🔴 Rose (`bg-rose-500`) - Low confidence/weak signal.

### 2. News Sentiment Score
A normalized assessment of recent headlines and news catalysts. In the backend, this score ranges from `-1.0` (extremely bearish) to `+1.0` (extremely bullish). In the frontend UI, it is converted to a simplified `0% to 100%` scale to match the aesthetic of the Decision Confidence bar.
- **Conversion Formula**: `Math.round(((score + 1.0) / 2.0) * 100)`
- **Color Mapping** (Symmetrical against neutral):
  - **`> 75%`**: 🟢 Emerald (`bg-emerald-500`) - Strong Bullish News.
  - **`56% - 75%`**: 🟠 Amber (`bg-amber-500`) - Mild Bullish News.
  - **`45% - 55%`**: ⚪ Slate (`bg-slate-400`) - Neutral / Mixed News.
  - **`25% - 44%`**: 🟠 Amber (`bg-amber-500`) - Mild Bearish News.
  - **`< 25%`**: 🔴 Rose (`bg-rose-500`) - Strong Bearish News.

### 3. Quantitative Targets
- **Entry Price**: The current or suggested market entry point.
- **Stop Loss**: A statically or dynamically calculated safety net.
- **Sell Target**: The projected profit-taking level.

### 4. Position Sizing & Leverage
- Displays the **Position Size** in USD alongside its percentage allocation of the portfolio.
- Suggests **Leverage** (e.g., `1x`, `2x`) based on current VIX regimes and volatility.

### 5. Contextual Summaries
- **🔥 Key Market Factor**: The single most dominant force driving the current price action (e.g., "Earnings beat," "RSI divergence").
- **📰 AI News Briefing & Catalyst Summary**: A text briefing synthesizing the latest headlines.
- **📝 Comprehensive Trade Thesis**: The full paragraph narrative from the AI explaining its decision.
- **⚡ Primary Catalyst & ⚠️ Key Risk Factors**: Two side-by-side modular cards providing the bull and bear case in bullet/short-sentence form.

## Architecture & Data Flow
1. The dashboard calls `/api/dashboard` or a direct fetch to `get_ai_indicator` via WebSockets/polling.
2. The Python backend processes market context in `ai_indicator.py` and returns a structured JSON map.
3. In `frontend/app.js`, `renderAIIndicatorCard(data)` parses the JSON, calculates the correct color styling variables based on the defined thresholds, and injects the HTML component over the DOM tree safely avoiding `null` references for missing fields.
